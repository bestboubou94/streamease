+
We support virtually every device — Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Android), Amazon Firestick, Apple TV, Android phones and tablets, iPhones, iPads, Windows PCs, Macs, and MAG Boxes.
Can I watch on multiple screens at the same time?
+
Yes! You can choose a plan with 1, 2, 3 or 4 simultaneous connections depending on your needs. You can install StreamEase on as many devices as you want — connections only limit simultaneous streams.

+
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you experience technical issues with our service, contact our support team who will first work to resolve the problem. If the issue cannot be resolved, a full refund will be issued.
Do I need a VPN to use StreamEase?
+
A VPN is not required. Our servers are secure and optimized for performance. However, if your internet provider limits streaming speeds, a VPN can help improve your experience.

Simply click any "Get Started" button on this page, choose your plan on WhatsApp, complete your payment and receive your credentials instantly. Our team will also help you with setup if needed.
